Hi, I’m Halcyon.

This is my little corner of the internet — a place for notes, projects, and things still taking shape.

I started this site because I wanted somewhere to gather what I am learning and making, especially the bits that do not quite belong anywhere else. A lot of it will live somewhere between mathematics, statistics, and AI, though I expect it to wander from time to time.

Some entries here will be polished. Many will not. I am more interested in keeping a thoughtful record than in pretending everything arrives complete.

If nothing else, I hope this becomes a quiet archive of ideas, attempts, and unfinished sketches.

Thanks for being here.
